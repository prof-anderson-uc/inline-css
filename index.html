Apollo: The British Shorthair Sensation

Apollo is a distinguished British Shorthair cat from the Loretta British Cattery in Chicago. He holds the prestigious title of Quadruple Grand Champion in The International Cat Association (TICA), a feat he achieved through five shows, showcasing his exceptional breed qualities. Apollo is celebrated for his plush, dense coat and striking sky-blue eyes—but his most iconic feature is his ridiculously huge, chubby cheeks, which fans absolutely adore.

Apollo’s fame extends far beyond the show ring. He’s a social media superstar with 600,000 followers on Instagram and over 400,000 subscribers on YouTube, where his daily antics and luxurious lifestyle capture the hearts of fans around the world. You can find him on Instagram: @lorettabritishcat

Whether he’s strutting through the city in his stroller or striking a pose for the camera, Apollo’s charisma is undeniable. His followers love his fashion sense, relaxed personality, and signature grumpy-chic look.

When he’s not lounging in his favorite sunny spot, Apollo enjoys a life of adventure and whimsy. His lifestyle is a blend of luxury and playfulness, making him a beloved figure among cat lovers worldwide.
